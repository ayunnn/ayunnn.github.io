学术五百词Group32

发表于2020-03-30|更新于2020-04-10|learning

Group32

  • interpret

  • intertwine

  • design

  • remedy

  • network

  • deplete

  • denigrate

  • detect

  • interact

  • reside

例句：

For nearly all of human history, most people have interacted exclusively(唯一地，仅仅) with others who are close at hand.

close at hand: 在附近，在旁边

There are several basic theoretical dilemmas — matters of continuing or recurring controversy or dispute — which these clashes of viewpoint bring to our attention, some of which concern very general matters to do with how we should interpret human activities and social institutions.

The British mathematician Alan Turing designed a primitive computer to assist British intelligence in breaking the secret codes of German ciphering machines.

Spiritual and secular (世俗的) affairs were closely intertwined in the sixteenth century.

In business, it is important to network with as many people as possible on a face to face basis.

Both campaigns depleted German manpower, while the Americans were arriving in great numbers to strengthen Allied lines and uplift morale(士气).

Prisoner-of-war camp guards would sometimes display good manners to captives in their first days on the job, but not for long. Soldiers ordered to kill may initially react with revulsion(厌恶) to the point of sickness over their act. But not for long (Waller, 2002). Before long, they will denigrate their enemies with nicknames. People tend to dehumanize(失去人性) their enemies and humanize their pets.

Can you detect a bias in this document?

Unlike Britain, the United States was a large country. The lack of a good system of internal transportation seemed to limit American economic development by making the transport of goods prohibitively expensive. This was gradually remedied, however. Thousands of miles of roads and canals were built linking east and west.

The state accepts the responsibility of protecting everyone who resides within its borders from illegitimate(非法的) violence.

单词释义：

interact: to act on each other

互相作用

interpret: to explain the meaning of; to understand the significance of

解释，说明

intertwine: to join or become joined by twining together

缠绕，纠缠

network: to interconnect as components in a group or system; to interact or engage in informal communication with others for mutual assistance or support

将…连成网络

deplete: to consume or reduce to a very low amount; to remove the contents or important elements of

耗尽，用尽

denigrate: to attack the character or reputation of; to disparage

诋毁，玷污

detect: to discover or ascertain the existence, presence, or fact of; to discern (something hidden or subtle)

察觉，发现

design: to formulate a plan for; to have as a goal or purpose; to create or contrive for a particular purpose or effect

设计，构思

remedy: to counteract or rectify (a problem, mistake, or undesirable situation)

补救，改进

reside: to live in a place permanently or for an extended period; to be inherently present

住，居住
