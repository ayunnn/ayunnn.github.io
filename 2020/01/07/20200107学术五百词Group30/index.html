单词释义：

deduce: to reach (a conclusion) by reasoning; to infer from a general principle
推论，推断

reduce: to bring down, as in extent, amount, or degree; to put in a simpler or more systematic form
减少，缩小

result: to happen as a consequence; to end in a particular way
结果

revise: to alter or edit (a text); to reconsider and change or modify
修订，校订

relate: to give an account of (an occurance, for example); to have connection, relation, or reference
涉及，认同

reinforce: to give more force or effectiveness to; to strengthen by adding extra support or material
加强，加固

reflect: to make apparent; to bring as a consequence
反映，表达

reveal: to make known (something concealed or unknown); to cause to be seen
揭露，显示

review: to consider retrospectively
回顾，复习

reorganize: to organize again or anew
整理
