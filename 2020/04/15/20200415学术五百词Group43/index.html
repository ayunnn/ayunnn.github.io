学术五百词Group43

发表于 2020-04-15 分类于 learning Valine：

Group 43

  • criticize
  • analyze
  • familiarize
  • formalize
  • normalize
  • harmonize
  • prioritize
  • globalize
  • rationalize
  • catalyze

例句：

The media industry has become globalized over the past three decades.

In an influential treatise(论述，论文) on painting, Poussin formalized the rules that would govern academic art for centuries.

“The human ability to normalize the abnormal is frightening indeed,” observes sociologist Rainer C. Baum.8 Mass murderers need not be psychopaths(精神病者). It is a “disturbing psychological truth,” states Robert Jay Lifton, that “ordinary people can commit demonic(恶魔的) acts.”

What, then, is the relationship between religion and prejudice? The answer we get depends on how we ask the question. If we define religiousness as church membership or willingness to agree at least superficially with traditional religious beliefs, then the more religious people are the more racially prejudiced. Bigots(偏执者) often rationalize bigotry with religion.

The Social Contract, published in 1762, Rousseau tried to harmonize individual liberty with governmental authority.

The goal of the experiment was to familiarize the people with the new laws.

Prioritize your own wants rather than constantly thinking about others.

Inspired by the Gospels, reformers criticized the church for its wealth and involvement in worldly affairs.

Persuasion is also enhanced by a distraction that inhibits counterarguing. Political ads often use this technique. The words promote the candidate, and the visual images keep us occupied so we don’t analyze the words.

That kind of self-examination is what you need in order to catalyze personal change and improvement.

单词释义：

globalize: to make global or worldwide in scope or application

全球化

formalize: to give formal standing or endorsement to; to make formal

使形式化

normalize: to cause(something previously regarded as anomalous) to be accepted as normal, thereby altering the accepted norm

使正常化，使标准化

rationalize: to explain rationally; to attempt to justify (one’s behavior) by providing reasons that obscure one’s actual motives

使合理化

familiarize: to make acquainted with

使熟悉

harmonize: to bring or come into agreement or harmony; to be in agreement

使和谐，一致

prioritize: to arrange or deal with in order of importance; to put things in order of importance

把…区分优先次序

criticize: to find fault with

批评，评论

analyze: to examine methodically by separating into parts and studying their interrelations

对…分析

catalyze: to bring about; to produce fundamental change in

催化，刺激，促进
