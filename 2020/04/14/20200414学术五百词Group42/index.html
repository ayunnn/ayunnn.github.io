单词释义：

intersect: to form an intersection with

横断，相交

subject: to cause to experience, undergo, or be acted upon; to subjugate

使臣服，使顺从

subject sb/sth to sth 使经受，使遭受

effect: to bring about

产生，达到目的

neglect: to pay little or no attention to; to fail to care for or attend to properly; to fail to do or carry out, as through carelessness or oversight

忽视，忽略

elicit: to call forth, draw out, or provoke (a response or reaction, for example)

抽出，引出

expend: to gay out; to use up

花费，消耗

append: to add as a supplement or appendix; to fix to

附加，贴上

tend: to have the care of; to manage the activities and transactions of

趋向，照料

erode: to cause to diminish or deteriorate

腐蚀，侵蚀

probe: to make an inquiry about (something); to pose questions or conduct an investigation

调查，探测
