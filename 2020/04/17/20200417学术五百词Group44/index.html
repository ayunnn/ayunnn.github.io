单词释义：

publish: to prepare and issue (a book, music, or other material) for public distribution, especially for sale; to issue a publication

出版，发行

partition: to divide into parts, pieces, or sections; to divide or separate by means of a partition; to divide (a country) into separate, autonomous nations

分割，区分

paraphrase: to restate using different words, especially to clarify

释义

precipitate: to cause to happen, especially suddenly or prematurely

使（坏事）等突然发生

proscribe: to prohibit

禁止

promote: to contribute to the progress or growth of; to urge the adoption of;to attempt to sell or popularize by advertising or publicity

propound: to put forward for consideration

提出，提议

ground: to provide a basis for (a theory, for example); to supply with basic information

研磨，压迫

pervade: to be present throughout

遍及，弥漫

degrade: to lower in quality or value; make inferior or less valuable; to lower in dignity
