学术五百词Group34

发表于2020-04-01|更新于2020-04-01|learning

Group34

  • concur
  • counteract
  • correspond
  • coordinate
  • coexist
  • exist
  • constitute
  • conserve
  • redress

例句：

The world constituted a single society of equal human beings.

If you are an American, would you favor higher gasoline and oil taxes to help conserve resources and restrain climate change?

There are many scientists who concur with Leshner, believing that science offers answers to questions such as “when?” and “how?”

The vertical accents, and the tall, narrow windows counteract and balance the horizontal elements.

The numbers correspond to points on the map.

Liberals feared that state intervention in the economy to redress social ills would disrupt the free market, threatening personal liberty and hindering social well-being.

The agencies are working together to coordinate policy on food safety.

God’s house, which we think of as one, is thus divided into three; some pray, others fight, and yet others work. The three groups, which coexist, cannot bear to be separated; the services rendered by one are a precondition for the labors of the two others; each in his turn takes it upon himself to relieve the whole.

Law enforcement exists in all countries, but comparative empirical studies of police forces are needed to reveal their similarities and differences

单词释义：

constitute: to be the elements or parts of

组成，构成

conserve: to protect from loss or harm; to use carefully or sparingly, avoiding waste

保存，保护

concur: to grant or concede; to be of the same opinion

同意，一致

counteract: to oppose and mitigate the effects of by contrary action

抵消，中和

correspond: to be in agreement, harmony, or conformity

符合，一致

redress: to set right (an undesirable situation, for example)

纠正，赔偿

coordinate: to cause to work or function in a common action or effort; to make harmonious

调节，配合

coexist: to exist together, at the same time, or in the same place; to live in peace with another or others despite differences, especially as a matter of policy

共存，和平共处
