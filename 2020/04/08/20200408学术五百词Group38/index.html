单词释义：

articulate: to express in coherent verbal form; to fit together into a coherent whole

发音，清楚地讲话

ameliorate: to make or become better

改善（与meliorate同义，差别很小）

precede: to come, exist, or occur before in time; to be in front of or prior to in order

领先，在前面

predict: to state, tell about, or make known in advance, especially on the basis of special knowledge

预言，预测

predate: to mark or designate with a date earlier than the actual one; to precede in time

在日期上早于，提早日期

predicate: to base or establish(a statement or action, for example); to state or affirm as an attribute or quality of something

使…基于，断定

predetermine: to determine, decide, or establish in advance; to influence or sway toward an action or opinion

预先决定

intermingle: to mix or become mixed together

混合

diminish: to make smaller or less; to become smaller or less

减少
