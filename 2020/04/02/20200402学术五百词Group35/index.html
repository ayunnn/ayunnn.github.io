单词释义：

advance: to cause to move forward; to aid the growth or progress of; to raise in rank

前进，提前

advocate: to speak, plead, or argue in favor of

提倡，拥护

adhere: to remain devoted to or be in support of something; to carry out a plan, scheme, or operation without deviation

坚持，依附

adjudicate: to make a decision(in a legal case or proceeding), as where a judge or arbitrator rules on some disputed issue or claim between the parties; to study and settle(a dispute or conflict)

裁定，宣判

adapt: to make suitable to or fit for a specific use or situation; to become adapted

适应，使适应，改编

ascribe: to regard as arising from a specified cause or source; to regard as belonging to or produced by a specified agent, place, or time

归因于，归咎于

ascertain: to discover with certainty, as through examination or experimentation.

确定，查明

modulate: to regulate or adjust to a certain degree

调节，调制

monitor: to keep track of systematically with a view to collecting information; to test or sample, especially on a regular or ongoing basis; to keep close watch over

监控

migrate: to move from one country or region and settle in another

迁移，移居

Note

migrate: 迁移，移居

emigrate: 移居国外

immigrate: 移民入境

词根 migr = to remove 迁移
