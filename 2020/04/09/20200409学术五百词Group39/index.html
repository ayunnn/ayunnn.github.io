学术五百词Group39

发表于2020-04-09|更新于2020-04-09|learning

Group39

  • attune
  • attribute
  • delineate
  • affiliate
  • appropriate
  • accentuate
  • estimate
  • interweave
  • devise
  • attempt

例句：

Since the early calls for immigration controls, housing has been at the forefront of struggles over resources between groups and tendencies towards ethnic closure. One reason for this may be that housing is a highly symbolic matter——it indicates status, provides security and interweaves with overall livelihood.

In math, the Sumerians devised a number system based on 60, using combinations of 6 and 10 for practical solutions.

As adults, women in individualist cultures describe themselves in more relational terms, welcome more help, experience more relationship-linked emotions, and are more attuned to others’ relationships.

Each apple has a powerful physical presence as it is built up out of slab like brushstrokes, its form also carefully delineated with a distinct line.

The parties to which people are affiliated express religious as well as class differences.

He is suspected of appropriating government funds.

A stairway and a colonnaded(有柱廊的) portico(门廊) accentuate the single entranceway and give the building a frontal “focus” usually lacking in Greek temples.

Learned helplessness often occurs when attempts to improve a situation have proven fruitless(徒劳的).

Cause and effect and the other categories of the mind permit us to attribute certainty to scientific knowledge.

Under any circumstances, mosaic(马赛克，镶嵌图案的) production was a costly enterprise; it has been estimated that it was at least four times more expensive than wall painting.

单词释义：

interweave: to blend together; to intertwine

交织，组合

devise: to form, plan, or arrange in the mind

设计，发明

delineate: to show or contain a distinguishing characteristic of; to draw or depict

描绘，描画

affiliate: to adopt or accept as a member, subordinate associate, or branch; to associate (oneself) as a subordinate, subsidiary, employee, or member

附属，加入

appropriate: to set apart for a specific use; to take possession of or make use of exclusively for oneself, often without permission

占用，拔出

accentuate: to stress or emphasize

强调，重读

attempt: to try to perform, make, or achieve

企图，试图

attune: to bring into a harmonious or responsive relationship

使协调，使合拍

attribute: to regard as arising from a particular cause or source

归属，把…归于

estimate: to calculate approximately(the amount, extent, magnitude, position, or value of something)

估计，估量
