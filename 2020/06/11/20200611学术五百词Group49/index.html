单词释义：

demarcate: to set the boundaries of; to separate clearly as if by boundaries

划分界限，区别

differentiate: to constitute the distinction between; to perceive or show the difference in or between

区分，区别

document: to methodically record the details of

记录，记载

purport: to have or present the often false appearance of being or intending

声称，意图，打算

highlight: to make prominent

突出，强调

weight: to assign weights or a weight to; to make heavy or heavier

加重量于，使变重

warrant: to provide adequate grounds for

保证，担保

supplement: to provide or form a supplement to

增补，附录

sanction: to give official authorization or approval to; to penalize

制裁，处罚

yield: to furnish as return for effort or investment; to give up (an advantage, for example) to another; to give forth by a natural process

产出，放弃，屈服
