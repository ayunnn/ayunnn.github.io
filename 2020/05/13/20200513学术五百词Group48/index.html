单词释义：

conceive: to apprehend mentally; to form or develop in the mind; to form or hold an idea

设想，构想

derive: to obtain or receive from a source

起源，源自

perceive: to become aware of (something) directly through any of the senses, especially sight or hearing; to achieve understanding of

察觉，感觉

archive: to place or store in an archive

把…存档

strive: to exert much effort or energy

stress: to place emphasis on

refrain: to hold oneself back
