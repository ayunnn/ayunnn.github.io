单词释义：

center: to have a central theme or concern

居中，集中

buffer: to act as a buffer (something that lessens or absorbs the shock of an impact) for or between

缓冲

confer: to bestow (an honor, for example)

协商，授予

hamper: to prevent the free movement, action, or progress of

妨碍，束缚

shape: to influence in a formative way; to direct the course of

形成

safeguard: to ensure the safety or integrity of

保护，保卫

stagnate: to be or become stagnant

停滞，淤塞

state: to set forth in words

规定，声明

stem: to make headway against; to have or take origin or descent

阻止；起源于某事物

deem: to regard as; to suppose or believe; to have an opinion

认为，视作
