单词释义：

inform: to impart information to; to give form or character to
通知，告诉

infuse: to fill or cause to be filled with something
灌输，使充满

index: to furnish with an index; to indicate or singal
指出，做索引

institutionalize: to make into, treat as, or give the character of an institution to
使…制度化

incorporate: to unite(one thing) with something else alreadly in existence; to give substance or material form to
包含，吸收，合并

initiate: to set going by taking the first step; to introduce to a new field, interest, skill, or activity
开始，创始，发起

innovate: to begin or introduce (something new) for the first time
创新，改变，创立

impede: to retard to obstruct the progress of
阻碍，妨碍，阻止

implicate: to involve or connect intimately or incriminatingly; to have as a consequence or necessary circumstance
牵连，涉及，表明…是起因

impute: to relate(something, usually something bad) to a particular cause or source; to assign as a characteristic
归罪于，归咎于

Notes
