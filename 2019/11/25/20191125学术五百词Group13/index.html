单词释义：

circumscribe: to draw a line around; to form or mark the limits of; to limit narrowly
限制，约束，画外接圆

compose: to make up the constituent parts of
组成

convert: to undergo a conversion
转变

complicate: to make or become complex or perplexing
使复杂化，使恶化

complement: to serve as a complement to
补足物

culminate: to come to completion; end
使结束，使达到高潮

contradict: to assert the opposite of a statement or idea put forward by (someone); to be contrary to
反驳，否认

reconcile: to settle or resolve; to bring (oneself) to accept; to make compatible
使一致，调解

cite: to quote or refer to (a book or author, for example) as an authority or example in making an argument
引用

concern: to have to do with or relate to; to be of interest or importance to
担心，忧虑
