单词释义：
enhance: to improve or augment, especially in effectiveness, value, or attractiveness
提高，增强

enable: to supply with the means, knowledge, or opportunity(to do something); to make feasible or possible
使能够，使成为可能

embody: to represent in bodily or material form; to make part of a system or whole
体现，使具体化

embed: to cause to be an integral part of a surrounding whole
栽种，使嵌入

emanate: to come or send forth, as from a source
产生，散发

emerge: to become known or prominent after being in obscurity; to come into existence
浮现，显现

emulate: to strive to equal or excel, especially through imitation
仿真，效仿

emphasize: to give emphasis to; to cause to appear important or deserving of attention
强调，着重

empower: to invest with power, especially legal power or official authority
授权，允许

employ: to provide work to (someone) for pay; to put (something) to use or service
使用，雇用
