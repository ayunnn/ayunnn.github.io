单词释义：

imbue: to relate(something, usually something bad) to a particular cause or source; to assign as a characteristic
灌输，使渗透

impel: to urge to action through moral pressure
推动，驱使，激励

imply: to express or state indirectly; to make evident indirectly; to involve by logical necessity
意味，暗示，隐含

import: to bring or carry in from an outside source, especially to bring in (goods or materials) from a foreign country for trade or sale.
进口，输入

impinge: to encroach on or limit something, such as a right
对…有明显作用，妨碍，侵犯

impart: to grant a share of; to make known
给予，传授，告知

tabulate: to arrange in tabular form; to cut or form with a plane surface
制成表格，使成平面

exacerbate: to increase the severity, violence, or bitterness of
使加剧，使恶化
