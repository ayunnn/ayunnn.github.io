单词释义：

clarify: to make clear or easier to understand; to clear of confusion or uncertainty
阐明，澄清，使清晰易懂

characterize: to describe the qualities or peculiarities; to be a distinctive trait or mark of
描绘…的特征，具有…的特征

circumvent: to avoid or get around by artful maneuvering
绕行，规避

cultivate: to improve and prepare (land), as by plowing or fertilizing, for raising crops; to grow or tend; to acquire, develop, or refine, as by education
培养，陶冶，耕作

alleviate: to make (pain, for example) less intense or more bearable; to lessen or reduce
减轻，缓和

allocate: to set apart for a special purpose; to distribute according to a plan
分配，拨出，使作用于

approximate: to come close to; be nearly the same as; to bring together, as cut edges of tissue
接近，近似

alter: to change or make different; modify
改变，修改

achieve: to gain with effort or despite difficulty; to succeed in accomplishing
取得，获得，实现

accrue: to accumulate over time; to come to one as a gain, addition, or increment
获得，积累
