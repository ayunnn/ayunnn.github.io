单词释义：

typify: to serve as a typical example of; to present by an imagem a form, or a model
代表，作为…的典型

unify: to make into or become a unit
整合，联合，统一

purify: to rid of impurities; to rid of foreign or objectionable elements; to free from moral or spiritual defilement
净化，使纯净

multiply: to increase the amount, number, or degree of; (Mathematics) to perform multiplication on
乘，繁殖，增加

specify: to state explicitly or in detail; to determine or bring about
指定，详细说明，例举

justify: to demonstrate or prove to be just, right, or valid; tp demonstrate sufficient legal reason for (an action taken)
证明…是正当的，证明合法

reiterate: to say or do again or repeatedly
重申

range: to move through or along or around in (an area or region); to vary within specified limits; to extend in a particular direction; to cover or have application to a number of things
在…内变动，归类于

group: to place or arrange in a group; to belong to or form a group
聚合，把…分组

Notes

今天学到一种表达，用identify表示从实验结果中发现的某种现象，可以用在论文results部分。比如，在支架长入实验中，A比B呈现出更多的血管，可以说“more vessels were identified in A than B”。
