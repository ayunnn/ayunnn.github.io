单词释义：

displace: to move, shift, or force from the usual place or position; to take the place of
取代，置换，转移

disentangle: to extricate from entanglement or involvement; to clear up or resolve (a plot, for example)
解开，松开，解决

distribute: to divide and dispense in portions; to deliver or pass out
分配，散布，分开

disseminate: to spread abroad; to become diffused
宣传，传播，散布

disallow: to refuse to allow; to reject as invalid, untrue, or improper
驳回，不接受

distinguish: to perceive as being different or distinct; to be an identifying characteristic of
区别，辨别

disperse: to drive off or scatter in different directions; to strew or distribute widely
分散，使散开

diverge: to go or extend in different directions from a common point; to depart from an established pattern or norm
偏离，分叉

dominate: to exert a supreme, guiding influence on or over; to be the most abundant in
控制，支配，占优势
