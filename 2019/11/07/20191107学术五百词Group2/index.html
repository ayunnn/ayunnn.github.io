单词释义：

conduct: to direct the course of; manage or control
引导，组织，实施，进行，表现举止

consist: to be made up or composed
由···组成
区分：consist of 由···组成或构成，consist in 存在于···
eg. The committee consists of ten members.
The beauty of the city consists in its magnificent buildings.

categorize: to put into a category or categories; classify
对···分类，把···归类

colonize: to form or establish a colony or colonies in
建殖民地，英式colonise

convey: to communicate or make known; impact
表达，传递（思想、感情等）；运送，传送

query: to put a question to (a person); to express doubt or uncertainty about
怀疑，询问

value: to regard highly; to rate according to relative estimate of worth or desirability
认为重要；估价
