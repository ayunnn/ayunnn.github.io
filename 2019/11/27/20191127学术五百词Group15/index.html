学术五百词Group15

发表于2019-11-27|更新于2020-03-08|learning

Group15

  • reconfigure
  • rectify
  • repress
  • couple
  • determine
  • reorder
  • redistribute
  • regress
  • redirect
  • render

例句：

Tax is both a way of banning certain transactions and redistributing income — you cannot pay someone a large income without it being the case that they have to hand some of it over for the state to redistribute to others.

The classical sociologists paid little direct attention to the environment, though others have tried to rectify that omission(疏忽).

In recent years, recognizing the shortcomings of the curricula(课程) inherited from colonialism, some developing countries have tried to redirect their educational programmes towards the rural poor.

It is unlikely that the court will render an opinion before November.

The whole system needs to be reordered.

The patient had regressed to a state of childish dependency.

We need to do reconfigure everyday objects, and our lifestyles, as our world downsizes around us.

Although these national groups began to favor the belief that each national group had the right to its own system of government, Metternich managed to repress the nationalist forces and hold the empire together.

All citizens of a polis(城邦) possessed basic rights, but these were coupled with responsibilities.

The Athenians also were determined to protect their city from enemies, but, unlike the Spartans, they valued political freedom and sought the full development and enrichment of the human personality.

单词释义：
reorder: to rearrange
重新整理

redistribute: to distribute again in a different way
重新分配

regress: to return to a previous, usually worse or less developed state; to have a tendency to approach or go back to a statistical mean; to move backwared or away from a reference point
退回，倒退

reconfigure: to rearrange the elements or settings of
重新配置

rectify: to set right; correct
改正

repress: to put down or subdue by force; to end, limit, or restrain, as by intimidation or other action
抑制，镇压

redirect: to cause to move in a different direction or go to a different destination; to give directions for an alternate destination to (someone); to change the object or focus of (attention)
使改方向，重定向

render: to give or make available; to cause to become; to represent in verbal form
提供；致使；表达

couple: to link together
结合，连接

determine: to establish or ascertain definitely, as after consideration, investigation, or calculation; to decide or settle (a dispute, for example) conclusively and authoritatively
决定，确定，判定
