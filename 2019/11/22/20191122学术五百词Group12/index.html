单词释义：

encompass: to have as part of something larger; include
包含，包围

endow: to provide with property, income, or a source of income; to equip or supply with a talent or quality
赋予，捐赠，天生拥有

enrich: to make fuller, more meaningful, or more rewarding
使充实，使肥沃，使富足

enlarge: to make larger in size; to make larger in scope or effect; expand
扩大，扩展

envisage: to conceive an image or a picture of, especially as a future possibility; to consider or regard in a certain way
设想，想象，正视

engender: to bring into existence; give rise to
产生，引起

elapse: to slip by; pass
流逝

elaborate: to work out with care and detail; to explain something at greater length or in greater detail
精心制作，详细阐述

eradicate: to tear up by the roots; to get rid of
根除，根绝
