单词释义：

address: to direct the efforts or attention of (oneself); to begin to deal with
设法解决，处理；致函；演说

abstract:to consider as separate from particular examples or objects
把…抽象出，提取

assign: to select for a duty or office; to set apart for a particular purpose or place in a particular category; to match or pair with
分配，分派，布置

aggregate: to amount to; total
总计，合计

isolate: to cause to become socially or politically unengaged or ostracized; to cause to be alone or apart, as in being inaccessible or unable to move about
隔离，孤立，脱离

resolve: to find a solution to; solve; to bring to a usually successful conclusion
解决（问题或困难），决心，作出决议
dissolve:溶解，解除，终止

socialize: to take part in social activities
交往，交际，使适应社会
~ with sb

dissociate: to remove from association; separate; to cease associating
否认同…有关系，声明无关；把…分开
dissociate oneself from 与……划清界限

associate: to connect in the mind or imagination; to correlate or connect logically or causally
联想，联系，交往

activate: to set in motion; make active or more active; to organize or create
使运作，使起作用，激活

Notes

今天写到“细胞受到细胞因子诱导”，想写诱导这个词，脑子里出现两个相似的单词：

induct & induce

简单来说，induct指正式就职，吸收为成员或者征召入伍等，通常后面会跟介词into。比如：In December he was inducted into the army.而induce指诱导，引起(此时后面跟介词to)，也特指引产。比如：The doctor induced labor.

induct

  • to formally or ceremoniously install in an office, position, et cetera.
  • to introduce into (particularly if certain knowledge or experience is required, such as ritual adulthood or cults).
  • to draft into military service.
  • to bring in as a member.

induce

  • to lead by persuasion or influence; incite.
  • to cause, bring about, lead to.

两者的名词形式分别是induction和inducement。
所以此处应该使用induce。
