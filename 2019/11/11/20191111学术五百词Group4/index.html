单词释义：

summarize: to make a summary or make a summary of
总结，概括

simplify: to make simple or simpler
使简化，使简易

span: to extend across in space or time
持续，贯穿，包括，涵盖

stipulate: to specify or agree to as a condition in an agreement; to agree to (a fact) in order to reduce the scope of the dispute to be resolved by a court.
规定，明确要求

systematize: to put into a system; arrange according to a plan or scheme
使系统化，使成体系

assimilate: to absorb (immigrants or a culturally distinct group) into the prevailing culture; to incorporate and absorb into the mind
吸收，同化，使接受，透彻理解

internalize: to make internal or cause to become internal; to take in and make an intergral part of one’s attitudes or beliefs; to direct (one’s bad feelings or conflicts) inwards, often as a manifestation of depression, anxiety, and social withdrawal
使（感情、态度或信仰）成为思想行为的一部分，使内在化

indicate: to suggest or demonstrate the necessity, expedience, or advisability of; to state or express briefly
表明，显示

institute: to establish, organize, or introduce
建立，制定，实行
