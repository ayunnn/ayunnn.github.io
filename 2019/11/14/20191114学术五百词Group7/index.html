单词释义：

constrain: to keep within certain limits; to compel by physical, moral, or circumstantial force
驱使，强迫，束缚

contain: to have within; to have as a component or constituent part; to hold or keep within limits
包含，控制，容纳

consolidate: to make strong or secure; to make firm or coherent
巩固，。加强

condense: to make more dense or compact; to make more concise, abridge or shorten
浓缩，凝结

compensate: to offset; to make satisfactory payment or reparation to
补偿，赔偿

compound: to add to or intensify so as to make worse; to make worse by being an additional or intensifying factor
合成，恶化，加重，妥协

explicate: to make clear the meaning of; explain
说明，解释

outreach: to surpass (another) in reach; to be more or greater than
超出，胜出

bracket: to furnish or support with a bracket or brackets; to place within or as if within brackets; to classify or group together; to include or exclude by establishing specific boundaries
用括弧括上；把…等同考虑，相提并论

locate: to place at a certain location; to become established
位于，定位
