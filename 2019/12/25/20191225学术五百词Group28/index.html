单词释义：
distort: to twist out of a proper or natural relation of parts
扭曲

display: to present to view; to be or give evidence of
显示，炫耀

discriminate: to make a clear distinction
区别，辨别

discern: to recognize or understand as being distinct or different
觉察，识别

disregard: to pay no attention or heed to
忽视，无视

predispose: to make (someone) inclined to something in advance; to make susceptible or liable
使倾向于

predominate: to dominate or prevail over
支配，主宰

presuppose: to believe or suppose in advance; to require or involve necessarily as an antecedent condition
假定，预料

propose: to put forward for consideration, discussion, or adoption
建议，打算，求婚

comprehend: to take in the meaning, nature, or importance of
理解，包含

Notes

technique 和 technology

technique多指某一领域（如艺术、体育等）的专门技术或技巧。
eg. She has fully mastered the technique.

technology含义比 technique 广泛，尤指生产工艺以及科学或工业技术。
eg. The new technology was applied to farming.
