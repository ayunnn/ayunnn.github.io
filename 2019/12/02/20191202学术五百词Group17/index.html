单词释义：
permeate: to spread or flow throughout
渗透

perpetuate: to cause to continue indefinitely; to prolong the existence of
使持续，使长久

pertain: to have reference or relevance; to belong as an adjunct, part, holding, or quality
关于，适用，从属

procure: to get by special effort
获得，取得

proliferate: to grow or multiply by rapidly producing new tissue, parts, cells, or offspring; to increase or spread at a rapid rate
增殖，扩散，激增

progress: to develop, advance, or improve
进步，发展

propagate: to breed(offspring); to cause to extend to a broader area or larger number; to make widely known
传播，繁殖

preclude: to make impossible, as by action taken in advance; to exclude or prevent(someone) from a given condition or activity
排除，妨碍，阻止
