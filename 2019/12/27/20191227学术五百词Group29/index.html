单词释义：

broaden: to make or become broad or borader
扩大，变宽

heighten: to raise or increase the quantity or degree of
提高

lessen: to make less
减少

encode: to convert (a message or other information) into code
编码

endeavor: to attempt (fulfillment of a responsibility or an obligation, for example) by employment or expenditure of effort
努力，尽力

engage: to attract and hold the attention of
从事，参与，吸引

enmesh: to entangle or catch in or as if in a mesh
使绊住，使陷入

encounter: to meet, especially unexpectedly; to experience or undergo
遭遇

entail: to have, impose, or require as a necessary accompaniment or consequence
牵涉，需要

tailor: to make, alter, or adapt for a particular end or purpose
定做，调整，迎合
