单词释义：
ally: to place in a friendly association, as by tready
联合，结盟

adopt: to take and follow(a course of action, for example) by choice or assent
采取，接受

allot: to parcel out; to assign as a portion
分配，拨给

arise: to come into being; to result
出现，产生，上升

amplify: to make larger or more powerful
放大，扩大，增强

augment: to make (something, already developed or well under way) greater, as in size, extent, or quantity
增加，增大

attain: to gain as an objective; to come to or arrive at
达到，获得

accumulate: to gather or cause to increase; to be the site for
积累
