单词释义：

integrate: to make into a whole by bringing all parts together; to join with something else
使完整

induce: to bring about or stimulate the occurrence of
诱导，引起

invoke: to appeal to or cite in support or justification
调用，祈求，引起

infer: to conclude from evidence or by reasoning
推断，推论

inhabit: to live or reside in
栖息，居住

inculcate: to teach(others) by frequent instruction or repetition
教育，反复灌输

instigate: to initiate or bring about, often by inciting; to urge on
唆使，煽动

irritate: to cause (someone) to feel impatient or angry
刺激，引起恼怒

illustrate: to clarify or explain, as by the use of examples or comparisons
举例，图解
