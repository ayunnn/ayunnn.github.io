单词释义：

centralize: to draw into or toward a center; to bring under a single, central authority
使集中

conflict: to be in or come into opposition
冲突，矛盾

conform: to be or act in accord with a set of standards, expectations, or specifications
符合，遵守

construe: to understand or explain the meaning of (something), especially in a particular way
分析，解释

construct: to form by assembling or combining parts
建造，修建，组成

condition: to cause to be in a certain condition
决定，使适应，以…为条件

contribute: to make a contribution
贡献，出力

consent: to give assent
同意，准许
